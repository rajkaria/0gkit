# Welcome to Show and tell 👋

Built something on 0gkit? Post it here — a weekend hack, a production service, a
demo video, a screenshot. Everything from "my first `0g storage upload`" to a
full app is welcome.

**A good Show and tell post includes:**

- **What it does** in one or two sentences.
- **A link** — repo, live demo, or video.
- **Which 0gkit pieces you used** — e.g. `0gkit-storage` + `0gkit-compute`
  (`router()`), or the `tee-attested-api` template, or a kit you applied with
  `0g add`.
- **One thing that surprised you** — good or bad. It's the most useful part for
  everyone else.

If you composed your app from kits (`0g add agent-memory live-feed`, …), say so —
it helps us see which kits are pulling their weight, and it's a great pointer for
the next builder.

Show and tell is also where we'll post release highlights and community
spotlights. Drop your build below. 🚀
